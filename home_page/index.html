Our Mission

Our mission is to collaboratively promote awareness of the arts and the sustainability of arts organizations in the Greater Westmoreland County region.

Westmoreland’s arts organizations are scattered and underconnected. GWAC brings them under one collaborative umbrella, increasing efficiency and visibility for everyone.
